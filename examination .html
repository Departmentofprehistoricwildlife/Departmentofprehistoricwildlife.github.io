Examination Protocol & Instructions

01

General Conduct

This is a closed-book, high-integrity examination. You are authorized to use the provided workstation only. Any attempt to access external data or communicate with other personnel will result in immediate termination of the application and possible disciplinary action under the Prehistoric Wildlife Act.

02

Examination Scope

The exam consists of twenty (20) intermediate to advanced level questions covering: Advanced Taxonomy, Paddock Ballistics, Sedation Pharmacokinetics, and Emergency Breach Management. A passing grade of 85% (17/20) is required for Division Clearance.

03

Time Management

Candidates are allotted exactly 20 minutes. The system will automatically lock and submit all recorded answers upon timer expiration. There are no extensions for "Slow Movers" or unexpected equipment failure.

04

Reporting Procedures

Upon completion, a formal Examination Report will be generated. This document is the property of the DPW and must be signed by the Director of Operations before Field Licensing is granted.
