Automated Data Purge Protocol

Reference: SEC-PURGE-FINAL-2026

Critical Authorization Warning

Initiating a data purge is irreversible. This protocol is intended to prevent sensitive genetic sequencing and containment telemetry from being accessed by unauthorized hostile entities or competing corporate interests during a total site compromise.

1. Trigger Conditions

A Level 5 Data Purge is automatically triggered or manually authorized under the following conditions:

  • Physical breach of the Primary Server Vault by non-biometric verified personnel.
  • Loss of contact with DPW Headquarters for more than 120 minutes during a Code Red event.
  • Manual activation by a Director-level agent using the physical "Dead Man's Switch."

2. Execution Sequence

01

Genetic Sequence Erasure

All proprietary DNA sequences, including the "TRX-Core" genome and hybrid blueprints, are overwritten with 7-pass random noise patterns.

02

Containment Log Destruction

Historical data regarding fence vulnerabilities, patrol routes, and sedative dosages are wiped to prevent tactical exploitation.

03

Hardware Decommissioning

Physical magnetic degaussers are activated in the server room, rendering all solid-state drives and magnetic media permanently unreadable.

3. Personnel Responsibility

During the purge sequence, all personnel must facilitate the destruction of physical assets. This includes the manual incineration of paper-based field notes and the destruction of local mobile tracking units.
