Legal Advisory for Sector Residents

Residents within a 50-mile radius of a DPW-designated "Sanctuary" are legally bound by the Containment Proximity Clause. This requires all inhabitants to maintain active internet connectivity for Emergency Portals and to follow all physical signage and acoustic directives during a breach event. Failure to adhere to these terms may result in the forfeiture of emergency medical assistance as outlined in the Medical Assistance Request documentation.
