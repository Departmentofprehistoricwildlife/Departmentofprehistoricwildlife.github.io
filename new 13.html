Mist Valley

CODE RED

Primary habitat for sub-adult Theropods.This valley has a High density of dense fog and rocky terrain.The valley floor is composed of jagged limestone pillars and deep crevices. These formations provide perfect ambush points for sub-adult Theropods and make 4WD vehicle navigation nearly impossible.

04

Azure Plains

SECURE

Open grasslands designated for Sauropod grazing.This area is Visible from Highway 12.12,000 acres of rolling shortgrass prairie and low-lying wetlands. The "Azure" name comes from the high mineral content of the Blueberry Creek run-off, which gives the watering holes a distinct turquoise hue.Under clear conditions, a Ranger on the Overlook Ridge can spot a herd of Brachiosaurs from up to 8 miles away.

PERIMETER: 42.8 KM

ASSETS: 45 (STABLE)
